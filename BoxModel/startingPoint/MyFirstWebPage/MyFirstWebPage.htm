SpiderPenguin

The world's first fish-powered superhero

About

Following an accident involving kelp, toxic waste, a nuclear submarine and a tasty tuna fish, mild-mannered Peter Penguin was transformed into the one and only SpiderPenguin - the world's first fish-powered superhero. Orcas quake and leopard seals quiver when this penguin goes for a swim.

SpiderPenguin's powers include casting webs, climbing walls, and catching fish in the special spider-nets he fires from his super-flippers.

Faster than a torpedo, stronger than a whale, and cooler than a cucumber, SpiderPenguin always saves the day
